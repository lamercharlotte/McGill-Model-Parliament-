🍁 McGill University · Student Association

Learn Parliament by stepping into it.

McGill Model Parliament is a student-run organisation that hosts beginner-friendly simulation games inspired by Canadian parliamentary procedure. We create an inclusive, entertaining, and professionally run environment where students from every academic background can engage more deeply with Canadian politics.

Simulation · Debate · Community

From first-timers to political enthusiasts.

Members learn the mechanics of Canadian politics by doing: debating motions, understanding parliamentary roles, and participating in an environment that values curiosity, respect, and fun.

Why students join

More than a club meeting.

  • Accessible programming that welcomes students who are completely new to politics.
  • Engaging simulation games that make parliamentary procedure easier to understand.
