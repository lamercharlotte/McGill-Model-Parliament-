About the organization

Grounded in debate, public service, and civic literacy.

McGill Model Parliament is a student-led organization dedicated to helping members understand parliamentary institutions, improve public speaking, and engage seriously with public affairs.

Our mission

We create an environment where students can develop confidence in speech, policy analysis, negotiation, and parliamentary procedure. Through simulations and structured training, members gain skills that transfer to academics, leadership, and public life.

What distinguishes MMP

The club is more than a debate space. Members learn how legislation is shaped, how caucus dynamics evolve, and how political ideas are tested under procedural pressure.
